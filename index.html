skin type

Choosing the right care for your skin type is the way to get soft and beautiful skin.

Select your skin care products based on the individual needs of your skin type.

oily

Oily skin needs to be careful of many things and special care. That way, your skin will be less shiny and you won't get troublesome. Light liquid or gel type is good, and moisturizers with soothing ingredients should be used. Avoid heat, friction, and strong cleansing.

normal

Your skin is soft, your pores are small, and your face looks good. If you see this face in the mirror, it's neutral skin. However moisturizing and protecting it is essential to maintain good skin. If you pay attention to eye care, your face looks bright.

dry

Do you have rough skin and dead skin cells? Dry skin lacks both moisture and lipids. That's why the skin loses its elasticity, it's tingling, and itches often. If you use a dry skin-only cream, you can restore moisture and strengthen your skin's.

complex

Complex skin tends to be shiny, especially the forehead, nose, and chin, which are called T-zone, are dangerous. In contrast, the cheeks are neutral or dry. You need to use products for complex skin to restore a broken moisture balance. Wash your face with lukewarm water instead of cold water or hot water.

sensitive

Sensitive skin is sensitive to external influences such as stress, dry air emitted by heaters, and ultraviolet rays. When the skin's balance breaks, the face becomes dry, itchy, and dry. Sensitive skin needs to increase resistance, so more special care is needed to calm the skin while strengthening the barrier.

troubled

Troubled skin is caused by genetic predisposition, stress, and hormones, and often skin problems occur frequently. Using specialized care products for troubled skin can help your skin regain balance. It can adversely affect your skin overnight, so make sure to remove your makeup in the evening.

men care

Men need to take care of them, too. Suitable care products for men's skin.

01

Thicker skin subcutaneous fat

A man's facial skin is different from a woman's in many ways. It's up to 30% thicker and contains a tighter network of fatty tissue called the subcutis. It also has higher water-binding properties which often make it appear firmer and tighter than women's skin. It's also less likely to dry out and form wrinkles. Word to the wise, never mention this to any of the ladies in your life.

02

A shaving wound

Men shave every day like a habit, but few people care as much as they do “skin care.” Men's skin is attacked by shaving every day. Many men suffer from skin irritation and unpleasant tightness after shaving. Not only are men's skin thicker, but they also support more collagen, which can keep them hydrated. But In the hot summer months, ultraviolet rays destroy skin collagen, so the skin condition is not good.

03

Testosterone Hormones

The feature of male skin is essentially larger and more sebaceous glands stimulated by testosterone hormones. This often makes men's skin greasier, larger holes, and more prone to blemishes than women's. Therefore, deep moisturizing facial creams, such as those frequently used by women as anti-aging treatments for dry skin, are not recommended for men. Instead, use cream that is high in moisture and low in fat. These creams improve your skin and provide immediate and complete care.
